About Me

I am an economist and data analyst from Mexico who uses data to address social issues such as violence, acces to justice and digital rights. I have more than six years of experience working with Mexican Non-governmental organizations doing data analysis and policy evaluation on violence and criminal justice.

I'm also the co-founder of Hijas de Internet (Internet’s Daughters), a platform that advocates for a safer, more diverse, and inclusive Internet for everyone.
